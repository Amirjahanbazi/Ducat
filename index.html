How Ducat Works
Four simple steps to unlock liquidity from your Bitcoin
01
Deposit BTC
Deposit your Bitcoin into a Ducat vault. Your BTC remains on Bitcoin Layer 1 in a non-custodial vault that you fully control. The protocol uses advanced Bitcoin scripting to ensure you always maintain ownership while enabling collateralized borrowing.
Non-Custodial Full Control L1 Native
02
Set Risk Preference
Choose your collateralization ratio based on your risk tolerance. Set conservative (160%+), moderate (145%), or aggressive (135%) ratios. Higher ratios provide more buffer against Bitcoin price volatility, while lower ratios maximize your borrowing power.
Flexible Risk Real-Time Monitoring Dynamic Adjustment
03
Borrow UNIT
Mint UNIT stablecoins against your BTC collateral, borrowing up to 62.5% of your vault value. Use UNIT anywhere in the Bitcoin-Fi ecosystem for trading, investing, or earning yield. When ready, repay your debt to unlock your BTC collateral without penalty.
Instant Access No Selling BTC Permissionless
04
Manage & Repay
Monitor your vault health in real-time through an intuitive dashboard. Track collateralization ratios, BTC price movements, and liquidation thresholds. Add collateral or repay UNIT at any time to maintain a healthy vault position and avoid liquidation.
Real-Time Tracking Health Indicators Automatic Alerts
Protocol Architecture
Built on Bitcoin's foundation with cutting-edge security
Bitcoin Runes Technology
DUCAT and UNIT tokens leverage Bitcoin Runes for efficient on-chain representation, ensuring native compatibility with Bitcoin's security model while maintaining programmability for DeFi operations.
Multi-Party Computation
A distributed MPC network manages vault operations without centralized control. Validators collectively secure the protocol through consensus-based decision making and cryptographic key sharing.
Canonical Reference Satoshis
Each UNIT token is backed by specific, traceable satoshis on the Bitcoin blockchain. This creates verifiable 1:1 mapping between stablecoins and their BTC collateral, ensuring full transparency.
Oracle Price Feeds
Decentralized oracle networks provide real-time BTC/USD price data, enabling accurate collateral valuations and triggering liquidations when necessary to maintain system stability.
DUCAT Governance
Protocol parameters, risk management, and upgrades are controlled by DUCAT token holders through distributed consensus. Ducat Nodes participate in governance decisions based on staked holdings.
Permissionless Architecture
Anyone can open vaults, mint UNIT, or participate as a liquidator without permission. The protocol operates transparently on-chain with all rules encoded in Bitcoin scripts.

Understanding Liquidations

  • When a vault's collateralization falls below 135%, it becomes eligible for liquidation
  • Liquidators can purchase the vault's BTC at a discount by repaying the UNIT debt
  • The 135% threshold provides a buffer against sudden BTC price drops
  • Liquidation tax funds protocol operations and incentivizes fast liquidations
  • The process is fully decentralized with no whitelisting or preferred liquidators
  • Profit-sharing mechanism increases as collateralization decreases, creating strong incentives
Liquidator Rewards
Earn immediate profits by recapitalizing distressed vaults. Purchase BTC collateral at discounts of 5-10% while maintaining protocol health. Competitive liquidation marketplace ensures efficient price discovery.
Protocol Stability
Automated liquidations maintain UNIT's dollar peg by ensuring all outstanding stablecoins remain overcollateralized. Economic incentives align liquidators with protocol health.
Profit Settlement
Liquidation profits are distributed transparently on-chain. The system calculates discount rates based on collateral shortfall, creating fair pricing while protecting the protocol.
Trust Assumptions
Understanding Ducat's security and decentralization model
Asset Management
Your BTC never leaves Bitcoin L1. Vaults use Bitcoin's native scripting with time-locks and multi-signature requirements. No bridges, no wrapped assets, no third-party custody at any point.
Decentralized Validation
MPC validator network operates without central authority. Threshold signatures require honest majority for operations. Validators are economically incentivized through DUCAT tokens.
Supply Control
UNIT supply is algorithmically controlled by on-chain collateral ratios. No entity can mint unbacked tokens. All issuance is transparent and verifiable on Bitcoin's blockchain.
